# CB-ROS
Catalyst selection cannot be retrieved — it must be derived.
We treat every experiment, especially the failed ones as a data "commit" that upgrades the entire system's intelligence. We move from simple prediction to Scientific Reasoning, where the system understands why a catalyst fails under specific conditions.
Our platform CB-ROS is designed as a Research Operating System (ROS) that:
1. reconstructs experimental knowledge
2. validates candidates through scientific constraints
3. enables human–AI co-creation
4. learns from both success and failure
